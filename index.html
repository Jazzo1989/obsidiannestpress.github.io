About the Book

In a realm where memory is stitched by divine threads, Jazaria Thorn—once erased by the Loom—returns as Vanta, a living glitch in the weave of fate. Her shadows do more than follow; they consume. Hunted by gods, feared by her own blood, Vanta carves a throne from the silence they forced upon her.

Prepare for betrayal, god-tier power, and a story that unspools with fury. If you like your fantasy dark, your sisters deadly, and your magic dripping with cosmic revenge, this saga is your next obsession.
